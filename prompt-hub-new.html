What is a Prompt?

A prompt is a set of instructions given to AI to get better, more accurate results. Think of it as the key to unlocking AI's full potential - whether you're learning, working, or creating. Proper prompts can boost your efficiency dramatically and help you achieve professional-quality outputs.
